Events

Tree Sale(20th-22nd)

Every year on the weekend before christmas we have a 50% off sale for those in need of a tree still. You may choose any tree that we have listed in our shop, but remember that this is a limited time deal and supplies may not last. It's best to act quick during this sale.

Free Trees!(23rd)

For those who can't afford a tree we give you an option which you can take part in on the day before Christmas. If you still need a tree then come on down and grab one for free for a limited time. The only condition is that you must bring your own chopping tool and vehicle to haul it with. We understand people struggle and we hope to bring some joy to the less fortunate.

Christmas Parade(21st)

We host an annual Christmas Parade every year and would love for you to be a part of it. There are currently 12 floats in the parade, but if you decide you want to be in it then you need to contact us by December 14th(1 week in advance) in order to get everything prepared in time.
